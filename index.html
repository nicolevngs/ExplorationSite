Living in-between two worlds

Nikkitriki appeared one day from the hands of Nicole Valdespino Nogues. She is the result of the crossing of ideas, introspection and many many existential questions. Alone in Nicole's room, Nikkitriki came to life. She tends to sneak into Nicole's daily life with strong opinions and heavy sarcasm. Through Nikkitriki, Nicole lives the conflicts of work, family, relationships, friends ... the feminine frustration before certain orthodox positions of some troglodytes and a lot, a lot of empowerment ready to be shared.

  • Protagonism

    Together they are stronger, like the wild sea. They feed each other mind and soul. They fight together for what they believe

  • Existence

    Living in-between two worlds could be perceived as being trapped. It is not! Nikkitriki is nothing but free. She lives through Nicole and Nicole lives through her.

  • Growth

    Nikkitriki is shy and is in constant transformation. She will never be silent against injustices. Through Nicole's hands she will shout. Nicole promised to always listen to her voice.
